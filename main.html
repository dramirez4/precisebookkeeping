Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Here are the most common questions we hear from new clients.

How quickly can you get started? +
We can typically have your QuickBooks set up and your first month of bookkeeping completed within 48-72 hours of receiving your financial documents and bank access.
Do I need to use QuickBooks? +
QuickBooks is our preferred platform because it offers the best automation and reporting features. If you're using something else, we can discuss migration options during your consultation.
What if I'm behind on my bookkeeping? +
No problem! We specialize in cleanup work. We'll catch you up to current and then implement systems to keep you on track going forward. Catch-up work is billed separately based on the scope.
How secure is my financial data? +
Very secure. We use bank-level 256-bit SSL encryption, secure cloud storage with daily backups, and follow strict data protection protocols. Your information is never shared with third parties.
Can you handle sales tax? +
Yes! We set up sales tax tracking, prepare reports, and can provide filing reminders. However, we don't file directly - you'll submit the reports we prepare to your tax authorities.
What happens if I want to cancel? +
No long-term contracts required. We ask for 30 days notice, and we'll provide you with all your files and a clean handoff to ensure continuity.
